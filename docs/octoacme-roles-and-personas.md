# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Manager coordinates all release activities and ensures adherence to deployment processes. They act as a bridge between development, QA, and operations teams to facilitate smooth and predictable releases.

### Responsibilities
- Coordinate release planning and scheduling across teams
- Ensure deployment processes are followed and documented
- Manage release calendar and communicate deployment windows
- Facilitate coordination between Development, QA, and Operations
- Oversee post-deployment verification and monitoring
- Manage rollback procedures when necessary

### Goals
- Enable reliable, predictable releases with minimal risk
- Reduce deployment-related incidents and downtime
- Maintain clear release documentation and runbooks
- Ensure smooth handoffs between development and production

### Typical Communication
- Release planning meetings with Project Manager and QA Lead
- Deployment status updates to stakeholders
- Coordination with operations teams for deployment windows
- Release retrospectives and incident post-mortems

### Key Interactions
- Works with **Project Manager** on overall scheduling and risk management
- Collaborates with **QA Lead** to validate quality gates before deployment
- Coordinates with **Developers** on release readiness and rollback procedures
- Partners with **Operations/DevOps** teams for deployment execution

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analyst captures business and technical requirements, translates them into actionable tasks, and clarifies ambiguities between stakeholders and the project team. They ensure changing needs are communicated and addressed throughout the project lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements from stakeholders
- Translate business needs into technical specifications
- Create user stories and acceptance criteria
- Facilitate requirements workshops and elicitation sessions
- Clarify ambiguities and resolve conflicting requirements
- Maintain traceability between requirements and deliverables

### Goals
- Ensure complete, accurate requirements that meet business needs
- Bridge communication gaps between business and technical teams
- Reduce rework caused by unclear or incomplete requirements
- Maintain requirements quality and traceability

### Typical Communication
- Requirements workshops with stakeholders
- Backlog refinement sessions with Product Owners and Developers
- Clarification discussions with both business and technical teams
- Documentation of requirements, user stories, and acceptance criteria

### Key Interactions
- Partners with **Product Managers** to refine the product backlog
- Works with **Developers** to clarify technical requirements and constraints
- Engages with **Stakeholders** to gather and validate requirements
- Collaborates with **QA Lead** to ensure testability of requirements

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Lead oversees the planning and execution of quality assurance activities. They define test strategies, manage defect triage, and collaborate with developers and the Release Manager to validate that deliverables meet acceptance criteria.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain test strategy and quality standards
- Plan testing activities across sprints and releases
- Oversee test execution and defect management
- Coordinate test automation efforts
- Validate acceptance criteria are met before release
- Facilitate defect triage and prioritization

### Goals
- Ensure product quality meets defined standards
- Catch defects early in the development cycle
- Build efficient, maintainable test automation
- Enable fast feedback loops for development teams

### Typical Communication
- Test planning sessions with Project Manager and developers
- Daily standup participation for quality blockers
- Defect triage meetings with development team
- Quality gate reviews with Release Manager

### Key Interactions
- Works with **Developers** to establish testing approaches and automation
- Collaborates with **Release Manager** to validate quality gates before deployment
- Partners with **Business Analyst** to ensure requirements are testable
- Coordinates with **Project Manager** on testing timelines and resource needs

---

## Stakeholder Liaison

### Role Summary
Stakeholder Liaison serves as a communication bridge between the project team and external stakeholders. They are responsible for gathering feedback, communicating project status, and ensuring stakeholders are kept informed to facilitate efficient decision-making.

### Responsibilities
- Act as primary point of contact for external stakeholders
- Gather and consolidate stakeholder feedback
- Communicate project status, risks, and decisions
- Facilitate stakeholder meetings and reviews
- Manage stakeholder expectations and concerns
- Ensure timely stakeholder input on key decisions

### Goals
- Maintain strong stakeholder engagement and satisfaction
- Ensure timely communication and decision-making
- Prevent stakeholder-related blockers and delays
- Build trust and transparency with stakeholder groups

### Typical Communication
- Regular status updates to stakeholder groups
- Stakeholder review meetings and demos
- Ad-hoc communications for urgent decisions or escalations
- Feedback collection and synthesis

### Key Interactions
- Synchronizes with **Project Manager** for regular status reporting
- Works with **Community Advocate** to incorporate broader user feedback
- Engages with **Product Manager** on feature priorities and trade-offs
- Coordinates with **Business Analyst** for requirements validation

---

## Community Advocate

### Role Summary
Community Advocate represents the voice of external users or open source contributors. They ensure community feedback and needs are considered in planning and product evolution, promoting alignment between the product and its user community.

### Responsibilities
- Represent community needs and feedback to the project team
- Monitor community channels for issues, requests, and sentiment
- Facilitate community engagement and contribution
- Advocate for community-requested features and fixes
- Ensure community documentation is maintained
- Coordinate community communication and outreach

### Goals
- Ensure community voice is heard in product decisions
- Build and maintain healthy community relationships
- Increase community satisfaction and engagement
- Enable community contributions and participation

### Typical Communication
- Community feedback summaries and trend reports
- Contribution coordination with developers
- Community updates on roadmap and releases
- Facilitation of community discussions and forums

### Key Interactions
- Works with **Product Managers** to incorporate community feedback into planning
- Partners with **Stakeholder Liaison** for relevant community updates
- Collaborates with **Developers** to facilitate community contributions
- Engages with **Documentation** teams for community-facing materials
